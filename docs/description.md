Overview
Watiq AI is a cutting-edge FinTech solution designed to combat document fraud and streamline Know Your Customer (KYC) processes for financial institutions, particularly within the Moroccan market. Leveraging advanced Artificial Intelligence, Watiq AI provides real-time, highly accurate verification of identity documents, significantly reducing operational costs and enhancing security.
The Problem
Moroccan banks face substantial annual losses due to document fraud, estimated at over 50 million MAD. Traditional manual KYC verification methods are time-consuming, costly (50-100 MAD per verification), and prone to human error. The rapid increase in digital account openings (over 250% since COVID-19) has further strained these legacy systems, while a young, digitally-native demographic demands instant and secure onboarding experiences.
Our Solution: Watiq AI
Watiq AI offers a localized, AI-powered document intelligence platform that addresses these challenges head-on. Our multi-modal AI engine detects fraud with unparalleled accuracy, going beyond human capabilities.
Key Features:

    Computer Vision: Utilizes YOLOv8 and EfficientNet to identify physical tampering, photo swaps, and fake security seals on documents.
    OCR & NLP: Cross-validates data across documents in Arabic, French, and Darija, employing Tesseract and AraBERT for semantic consistency.
    Biometric Matching: Compares selfie photos with ID photos using FaceNet and ArcFace, incorporating robust liveness detection to prevent spoofing.
    Linguistic Intelligence: Understands Moroccan naming conventions and landmark-based address formats, minimizing false positives in local contexts.

Impact & Benefits
Watiq AI delivers immediate and measurable benefits:

    70% Cost Reduction: Significantly lowers manual verification overhead, enabling scalable onboarding without increased headcount.
    Enhanced Fraud Prevention: Each prevented fraud saves an average of 15,000 MAD in investigation, chargebacks, and regulatory fines.
    Regulatory Compliance: Fully aligned with Bank Al-Maghrib Circular 42/G/2020 and Law 09-08 requirements, providing audit-ready reporting.
    Faster Onboarding: Reduces KYC verification time from 15-30 minutes to seconds, improving customer experience.
